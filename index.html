What If the Buddha Had Been a Woman?

What if the Buddha had been a woman?
She leaves her sleeping child to seek the truth.
And the world calls her selfish — not enlightened.

It’s a question that cuts deep. Not into doctrine, but into the collective bias we’ve inherited...

“We all have the same potential. The Buddha gave equal opportunity to men and women.”

In Buddhism, what matters is intention...

  • Tārā...
  • Prajñāpāramitā...
  • Yeshe Tsogyal...
  • Maha Pajapati Gotami...

And maybe, just maybe — she wasn’t running away. She was walking toward truth.

Written with inspiration from the teachings of His Holiness the 14th Dalai Lama.
